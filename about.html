Who we are

Darrens Garage Services is a family-run independent garage based on Belmore Road in Eastbourne. We've built our reputation the old-fashioned way: honest advice, quality workmanship and fair prices.

Whether it's a routine service, complex diagnostics or an unexpected repair, we treat every car as if it were our own — and every customer like a neighbour, because most of them are.

Most of our work comes from repeat customers and their recommendations — the kind of trust you only earn by doing the job properly, explaining things clearly and charging what's fair.
